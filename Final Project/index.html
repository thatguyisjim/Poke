Gym Puzzle

First step

The challenger enters the gym and is given a Wacan Berry. The Gym puzzle starts with the Challenger needing to reconnect a circuit in complete darkness. With each circuit part being connected, lights flicker and give off a small bit of light.

Once the challenger has completed this, they may move to the next room.
Second step

The challenger then enters the second room. Upon entry, they are presented with a ceiling of stars. They must battle trainers to reveal the constelation of a pokemon. If at least one of their pokemon has a super effective type against the pokemon shown, they may proceed to the final room to battle the gym leader.

This then allows them to battle Xero.
